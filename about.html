About The Composer

Kyle Snow is an American composer whose works appeal to both casual and traditional audiences as well as to seasoned listeners. Kyle holds a BM in Music Composition from the University of Florida where he studied under Drs. Paul Richards, Paul Koonce, James Paul Sain, and Peter Van Zandt Lane, and is currently pursuing his Master’s degree at the University of North Texas.

Kyle’s compositional repertoire currently includes pieces written for a variety of ensembles including, but not limited to, clarinet choir, saxophone quartet, string orchestra, wind quintet, wind band, and choir, with the promise of many more works to come.
